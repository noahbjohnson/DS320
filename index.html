An Analysis of Biased News

Completed by Noah B Johnson for DS320 at Luther College

The dangers of biased news sources is often discussed...

But can we quantify it?

In this report I analyze two biased news sources, one liberal, and one conservative. By exploring many of the questions surrounding the phenomenon of "fake news", hopefully we can gain a better understanding of the echo-chamber effect and how political bias and tone interact in the new media.
